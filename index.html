What is this Site?

Its a public journal, or place where I dump whatever is stuck in my mind. Simply the possibility, however slim, that a person could read this, changes the way I write it. I've found that I journal more often if I make it "public" (I am not SEO-optimizing this site, even though that would be funny).

Why is this Site?

I need a way to let go of some, well alot, of shit. And I do mean need; I have to get these thoughts, ideas, words out somewhere, anywhere or I think they will drag me back down. And I can't go back to that all-consuming void; I would die before that. And I don't want to die, so here I am. As you can see, some of it is going to be rather heavy and angsty, so you know, be warned.
