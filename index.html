What is this Site?

Its a public journal, or place where I dump whatever is stuck in my mind. Simply the possibility, however slim, that a person could read this, changes the way I write it. I've found that I journal more often if I make it "public" (I am not SEO-optimizing this site, even though that would be funny).

Why is this Site?

I need a way to let go of some, well alot, of shit. And I do mean need; I have to get these thoughts, ideas, words out somewhere, anywhere or I think they will drag me back down. And I can't go back to that all-consuming void; I would die before that. And I don't want to die, so here I am. As you can see, some of it is going to be rather heavy and angsty, so you know, be warned.

I wonder what love feels like

Is it warm like dawn sunlight, or does it embrace you like a comfy chair? Does it tickle like a rough kitten's tongue? Does it sparkle and glint like a pond gently kissed by a summer breeze? Is it fortifying like a cold shower in the morning? Is it sickly sweet like the 100th piece of candy you ate on Halloween? Does it shift and flow like the branches of a willow in a storm?

Would I be able to look past my disdain for the faceless, nameless humanity that abandoned me. The humanity that demanded that I heal myself, that I make myself whole before I would be allowed to Sure, the actual person in front had no way of helping me in the past, but I doubt they would have even if they did have the chance.

Why wasn't enough for anyone?

I've always been alone, the main reason being I was dead, empty husk of a barely alive human being. But that isn't the only reason, or at least, I hate it if it is the only reason.

I can look back and see how people kinda tried to approach me. But it was clear they were not approaching me as I was or close to how I was. They were approaching who they thought I was, apparently a confident, if quiet, attractive man. But that wasn't me; I was a scarred, drowning, empty husk of a human being.

Why did you give me a ham sandwich when I was bleeding out?

I wasn't hungry, I needed something to stop the bleeding. I needed to be rushed to a hospital, I needed you to hold my hand when they rushed me to the operating room. Did you not see I was dying, or did you just not care? I gave you what I could, as little as it was. Why could you not give a single piece from your overflowing abundance?

Why did I have to be seen That Way?

People have always treated me weirdly. They are nice, but distant. They say I am unapproachable, but they never tried. They act like I am going to bite their head off if they ask me a single question.

I never expected to get to this point, and its really fucking with me

I feel like a prisoner whose spent 20 years on death row, who suddenly get released. I had resigned myself to my fate; I was marked for death, that was that. I would never taste any number of pleasures, I had accepted my loss.

Why did I need to put all the effort into starting a relationship?

I was really fucked up, like really really fucked-up. If you were also a broken husk of a human being, I offer no judgment whatsoever; we understand each other. My anger is targeted at you, the okay people. The people whose worst thing in their life was not having friends for 3 weeks, you fuckers. The fuck do you expect me to break my back to get a sliver of your attention? While you live in an opulence I have no comprehension of? Fuck you, and your expectations!

I have no where to rest anymore

I used to be able to drown myself in TV and movies. I used to be able to recover some energy alone, locked in my room. But I can't do that anymore. I need to connect with people, but I don't where to meet them. I need to see a smile grow on the face of someone I care about.
