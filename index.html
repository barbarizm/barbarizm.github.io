What is this Site?

Its a public journal, or place where I dump whatever is stuck in my mind. I've found that I journal more often and more clearly if I make it "public". Simply the possibility, however slim, that a person could read this, changes the way I write it.

Why is this Site?

I need a way to let go of some, well alot, of shit. And I do mean need; I have to get these thoughts, ideas, words out somewhere, anywhere or I think they will drag me back down. And I can't go back to that all-consuming void; I would die before that. And I don't want to die, so here I am. As you can see, some of it is going to be rather heavy and angsty, so you know, be warned.

I have Complex-Post-Traumatic-Stress Disorder

Complex PTSD: From Surviving to Thriving is the seminal text on CPTSD, at least to my knowledge.

I wonder what love feels like

Is it warm like dawn sunlight, or does it embrace you like a comfy chair? Does it tickle like a rough kitten's tongue? Does it sparkle and glint like a pond gently kissed by a summer breeze? Is it fortifying like a cold shower in the morning?

Why wasn't enough for anyone?

I've always been alone, the main reason being I was dead, empty husk of a barely alive human being. But that isn't the only reason, or at least, I hate it if it is the only reason. I can look back and see how people kinda tried to approach me. But it was clear they were not approaching me as I was or close to how I was. They were approaching who they thought I was, apparently a confident, if quiet, attractive man. But that wasn't me; I was a scarred, drowning, empty husk of a human being.

Why did you give me a ham sandwich when I was bleeding out?

I wasn't hungry, I needed something to stop the bleeding. I needed to be rushed to a hospital, I needed you to hold my hand when they rushed me to the operating room. Did you not see I was dying, or did you just not care? I gave you what I could, as little as it was. Why could you not give a single piece from your overflowing abundance?
